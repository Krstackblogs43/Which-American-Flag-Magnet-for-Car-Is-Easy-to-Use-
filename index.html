Tips for Safe Removal and Storage

Keeping your USA flag magnets in top condition is easier than it seems. A few simple habits go a long way:

1. Remove Before Car Washes
Automatic car washes can dislodge magnets or damage the edges. Hand washing the magnet separately or removing it before a wash helps prolong its life.

2. Avoid Extreme Heat
Prolonged exposure to high temperatures can weaken magnetic strength. Try to park in the shade when possible or remove the magnet during heat waves.

3. Store Flat and Dry
When not in use, store magnets on a flat surface in a dry area. Avoid stacking multiple magnets, as they can stick together unevenly and cause warping.

4. Clean the Car Surface First
A clean, smooth surface ensures maximum adhesion. Wipe the car with a soft cloth to remove dirt or dust before placing your magnet.

5. Lift, Don’t Slide
Always lift the magnet off the car instead of sliding it. Sliding can scratch the paint over time, even with a protective backing.

FAQs

Q: Will the magnet scratch my car’s paint?
A: No, as long as the surface is clean and the magnet is used correctly, it won’t scratch the paint. Always lift the magnet instead of sliding it to prevent marks.

Q: Can magnets be used on all cars?
A: Magnets work best on steel or metal surfaces. Aluminum, fiberglass, or plastic body panels may not hold magnets securely.

Q: How do I clean my magnet?
A: Wipe with a damp cloth and mild soap if needed. Avoid harsh chemicals, as they can damage the finish or the magnetic backing.

Q: Are these magnets weather-resistant?
A: Yes, high-quality magnets are designed to withstand sun, rain, and wind without fading. Removing the magnet during extreme weather is recommended for longevity.

Q: Can I use a magnet on my car permanently?
A: Magnets are removable by design. For permanent attachment, a decal or paint is required. Using magnets temporarily ensures your car’s finish remains protected.

Q: How do I store magnets when not in use?
A: Store flat in a dry area. Avoid stacking them directly on top of each other without protective separation to prevent warping.
